About

Building better futures

Harnessing innovation to accomplish more

Our homes are manufactured offsite, and then transported for final assembly. This ensures complete control at every step, with minimal time onsite and greatly reduced wastage of materials, manpower and energy.

Because our structures are engineered in a fully-controlled environment, nothing is left to chance – so we can guarantee the quality of every detail.

Building on a legacy of premium Scandinavian homes

We are a Dubai-based homebuilder and partner of Sweden’s Surewood Housing AB, one of the world’s leading producers of engineered wooden buildings. Together, we have a seventy-five-year legacy of constructing premium homes.

Smarter in every way

Our proven engineering processes combined with decades of experience means we can guarantee that our homes will be finished within the agreed timelines at competitive costs.

While the outstanding quality of each home ensures it will last for generations to come.

Leadership team

Kristian Petersson

Founder and Chairman

Born and raised in Gothenburg, Sweden, Kristian is a qualified mechanical engineer with a postgraduate qualification in business administration and economics. Today, he is Chairman of KPS, one of the largest independent interior construction groups in the Middle East.

Viktor Petersson

Director

A Swedish born, third-generation Dubai resident and finance professional, Viktor has worked with both public and private sectors across healthcare, financial services, and energy. Before focusing on the family business, Viktor was an M&A consultant at PwC.

Philip Ebbersten

Director

Philip is a Swedish executive management professional with deep international experience of establishing operations and working across all aspects of a business, from business development and funding, through to organisational structures and day-to-day operations.

Fredrik Anheim

Chief Executive | Surewood Housing AB

Frederik is an experienced senior manager with deep experience of the construction industry. His skills include coaching, lean construction, construction management, and project management. He graduated in production management from Luleå Technical University in Sweden.

Klas Scherdén

Project Director

Klas heads Teknikkonsult, a project management company, and has a strong history of project management across several leading Swedish companies including construction group Flexator AB. He studied at Chalmers, a progressive university in Gothenburg, Sweden.

Per Modig

Per is a qualified electrician with a diploma in economics. He built his career in the Swedish construction sector, with a focus on modular construction and the assembly of single family homes. He now heads our Swedish production facilities at Surewood Housing AB.

KP Confidencia

Kingshaus® is owned by KP Confidencia, a UAE-based holding and leading investor in Middle Eastern companies since 1992. Confidencia conducts its business operations in related industries and in markets with long-term growth potential.

It is an engaged owner who works actively through the boards of its companies to drive innovation, efficiency, and continuous improvement in performance. Business ethics and governance are the foundation of the Confidencia ownership model.

Find out more at kpconfidencia.com.

KPS

Kingshaus is the sister company of KPS, a technology-led and design-focused interior contractor. Over the past thirty years, KPS has transformed over 20 million square feet of space.

With a presence in nine countries across Europe and the Middle East, KPS has an annual turnover of US$150 million and over 500 employees. KPS relishes a challenge and the feeling of accomplishment that comes when it solves a problem that others said couldn’t be solved.
