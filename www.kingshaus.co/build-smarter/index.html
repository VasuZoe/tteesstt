Build smarter

A better approach to building homes

We pre-construct our homes offsite

We build homes offsite in quality-controlled environments to save valuable resources, including time, energy, and manpower.

It allows us to maximise efficiency and deliver energy efficient homes at the highest quality in under six months, with minimal wastage.

Partner with us

We manage every aspect of homebuilding

From planning to final handover, we manage turnkey home construction at the highest international standards. This end-to-end control means we guarantee price, product quality and delivery time.

Design & Planning
From planning and designing one villa to entire residential developments, we partner with leading international architects.
Offsite production
We build villas, townhouses and four-story apartment buildings using modular and frame-based, structural platforms.
Logistics
From our facilities in Sweden, and soon also in the UAE, we manage every aspect of shipping and logistics.
Onsite assembly
Our skilled engineers assemble the modules onsite quickly and efficiently, with far less labour required than traditional construction methods.
Completion & Delivery
From the finest interior finishes to kitchens and landscaping, we have everything covered.

The right balance between speed, quality and cost

You no longer need to compromise between speed and quality, or even cost. We deliver the highest-quality homes in only four to six months. That’s over twice as fast as traditional construction methods. And the costs are practically the same.

Engineered to perfection

Frame

The exterior walls consist of sturdy wooden joists and dimensionally stable boards.

Insulation

Multiple layers of insulation ensure homes always stay warm or cool, as required.

Ventilation

Protective vapour barriers allow for breathability and a better indoor climate.

Panelized or modular

We produce houses from two different platforms. Flat modules for single family homes and volumetric modules for apartment buildings.

Panelized
Our multilayered panels for two-storey villas and townhouses are produced offsite, then transported flat and assembled onsite.
Modular
Using box type modules we can build townhouses and apartment buildings of up to four storeys, quickly and efficiently.

Renewable
resource

Our Swedish pinewood is a renewable resource and fully certified by the Forest Stewardship Council.

Strong

Wood is lightweight with a high strength-to-weight ratio. It can be treated to further enhance its resistance to environmental factors.

Versatile

Wood is a versatile material. It is easier to make alterations to the building in the future, if needed.

Safe

Our wooden frames are well-protected to ensure they maintain their load-bearing capacity and stability in the event of a fire.

Energy-efficient

Wood saves energy during construction. It is also an excellent insulator and does not retain heat or cold, so our homes also save energy over their lifetime.

Sustainable

Trees absorb carbon dioxide through photosynthesis during growth. Building with wood traps carbon and helps to counteract the greenhouse effect.

The future of home construction

Our technologically advanced facilities in Sweden can produce around 500 villas, townhouses, or apartment buildings each year.

Our semi-automated production lines manufacture structural elements as modules, including walls, beams, and roofing. New facilities will soon be added in Dubai.
